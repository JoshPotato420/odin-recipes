Filipino Mango Float

This no-bake dessert is a delightful combination of creamy whipped cream, sweet mangoes, and crunchy graham crackers. It's easy to make but requires chilling overnight for the perfect texture. So worth the wait!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups heavy cream, chilled overnight
  • Â½ cup condensed milk, chilled overnight
  • Pinch of salt
  • 4 ripe mangoes, chopped or sliced
  • 2 packs graham crackers

Steps

  1. Prepare the Mangoes: Ensure your mangoes are ripe and sweet. Peel, then chop or thinly slice them. Set aside.

  2. Whip the Cream:In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Gradually add the salt and condensed milk, then continue whipping until stiff peaks form.

  3. Assemble the Float: Crush the graham crackers to your desired consistency, or leave them whole. In an 8x8 inch glass container, layer the ingredients: graham crackers, whipped cream, and mangoes. Repeat once more, finishing with whipped cream and mangoes. Optionally, decorate with additional graham cracker crumbs.

  4. Chill or Freeze: Cover the container and refrigerate overnight for a soft texture or freeze overnight for an ice cream-like consistency. Thaw for 30 minutes before serving, adjusting the time based on your desired texture.
